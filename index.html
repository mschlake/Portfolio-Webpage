About me

It was this belief that led me to pursue a career in web development. Our organization needed someone to maintain our website, so I volunteered (with zero experience) and fell in love with coding.

I originally learned basic HTML and CSS through online videos and tutorials. The further I went down the rabbit hole, the more I wanted to learn. This led me to pursue a more structured curriculum in Web Development through Bloc.io.
